Frequently Asked Questions

What is JourneyCrew?
JourneyCrew is a group travel coordination app for families and friends. It helps your crew plan trips together, share itineraries, keep messaging organized, and stay in sync while you travel.
Who is JourneyCrew for?
Any group that travels together: multi-family vacations, friend trips, reunions, sports teams, meetups, and more. If you have more than two people on a trip, JourneyCrew makes it easier.
Which platforms will you support?
We’re starting with an iOS app, then expanding based on feedback. Joining the waitlist helps us prioritize which platforms and features to ship next.
How will you use my email?
We’ll only use your email to send occasional product updates and an invite when JourneyCrew is ready for you to try. No spam, no selling your data, ever.
Is JourneyCrew free?
During the early beta period, JourneyCrew will be free for testers. We’ll share future pricing and premium features with you long before anything changes.
